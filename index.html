Our Foundation

The Vision of Logan Westlake

Founded by Logan Westlake, the LW Foundation was born out of a refusal to accept the status quo. Driven by a deep-seated frustration with the neglect of our local thoroughfares, Logan took action.

"I couldn't stand seeing how dirty and filthy our roads were getting. Our community deserves better, so we decided to be the ones to change it."

— Logan Westlake, Founder

Today, the LW Foundation stands as a symbol of community accountability. We don't just wait for change; we drive it. Our partnership with the Hernando County Adopt-A-Road program ensures that our efforts are structured, legal, and high-impact.

Our focus is on more than just "picking up trash"—we are reclaiming the natural beauty of Hernando County for every resident and visitor.

Volunteer With Us

Real change requires boots on the ground. We provide all necessary safety equipment, including vests, gloves, and collection tools. Whether you are looking for community service hours or simply want a cleaner neighborhood, we welcome your help.

SAFETY FIRST

All volunteers must attend a brief safety orientation at the start of each project site cleanup.

GEAR PROVIDED

High-visibility vests, industrial gloves, and trash pickers are supplied to all participants.

By submitting, you agree to be contacted regarding LW Foundation cleanup events.
