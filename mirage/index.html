MIRAGE AVE

Our Mirage Ave initiative is dedicated to the systematic preservation of Hernando County's infrastructure. We focus on intensive roadside cleanup, invasive debris management, and long-term environmental stewardship to ensure this corridor remains a safe and clean passage for our community.

Litter Removal

Bi-weekly sweeps targeting micro-plastics and large-scale debris to prevent environmental contamination.

Safety Monitoring

Identifying and reporting road hazards and illegal dumping activities to local authorities.

Community Impact

Restoring local pride by maintaining aesthetic standards and supporting local ecosystem health.
